Your Trusted Construction Partner

Dekontractors Nig. Ltd is a premier construction company and general contracting firm based in Jos, Plateau State, Nigeria. With our headquarters strategically located at 29/10 Bauchi Road, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the construction industry.

Founded on principles of engineering excellence, structural integrity, and unwavering commitment to quality, we specialize in a wide range of construction services. Our portfolio includes residential buildings, commercial complexes, industrial facilities, and infrastructure projects.

Chief Executive Officer

Bldr. Jabir Shehu Na'Allah (MNIOB)

With over 15 years of experience in the construction industry, Bldr. Jabir Shehu Na'Allah brings extensive expertise in project management, structural engineering, and construction supervision to Dekontractors Nig. Ltd.

As a registered member of the Nigerian Institute of Building (MNIOB), he ensures that all projects adhere to the highest professional standards and regulatory requirements. His leadership philosophy centers on integrity, quality craftsmanship, and client satisfaction.
