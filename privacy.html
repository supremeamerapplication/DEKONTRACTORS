Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: At Dekontractors Nig. Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information when you:

  • Submit our contact form
  • Contact us via WhatsApp through our website
  • Communicate with us via email or phone
  • Voluntarily provide information in any communication

Personal Information

Name, email, phone number

Project Information

Service interests, project requirements

Communication Data

Messages, inquiries, correspondence

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests for information
  • Provide information about our construction services
  • Communicate project updates and proposals
  • Improve our website and services based on feedback
  • Maintain business records as required by law
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and ensure security

Important Note

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is only used for legitimate business purposes related to construction services.

3. Information Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Methods

  • Contact Form Submissions: Processed via WhatsApp - we do not store form submissions on our servers
  • WhatsApp Communications: Subject to WhatsApp's privacy policy and security measures
  • Email Communications: Stored securely in our business email system
  • Business Records: Maintained as required by Nigerian business regulations

3.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure communication channels for sensitive information
  • Regular security assessments of our systems
  • Employee training on data protection principles
  • Access controls to limit internal access to personal information

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

WhatsApp Business

Used for communication. WhatsApp's privacy policy applies to messages sent through their platform. We encourage you to review WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

Unsplash

Provides stock images displayed on our website. Unsplash's privacy policy applies to their image services.

Google Fonts & Font Awesome

Used for typography and icons. These services may collect usage data according to their respective privacy policies.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage for:

  • Theme Preference: Remembering your dark/light mode selection
  • User Experience: Improving website performance and preferences

We do not use tracking cookies or collect browsing behavior data. The local storage data is only used to enhance your experience on our website and is not shared with third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for communications at any time

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 8. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Active Communications: For the duration of our business relationship
  • Business Records: As required by Nigerian business laws (typically 6 years)
  • Marketing Communications: Until you unsubscribe or request removal
  • Inactive Accounts: Archived after 2 years of inactivity
